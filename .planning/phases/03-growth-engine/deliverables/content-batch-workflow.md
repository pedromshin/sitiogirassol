# Content Batch Production Workflow: Sitio Girassol

**Date:** March 2026
**Requirement:** INSTA-03
**Builds on:** Phase 2 Instagram Content Strategy (Section 5: Batch Filming Guide)
**Time commitment:** ~5-6 hours every 2 weeks (fits within 10-15 hrs/week total constraint)

---

## 1. Workflow Overview

The batch production model replaces daily filming with two concentrated production sessions per month. Each session produces 6-8 edited Reels covering 2 weeks of posting at 3-4 Reels/week.

### 5-Step Production Cycle

```
PLAN (30 min) --> FILM (2-3 hrs) --> EDIT (1.5-2 hrs) --> SCHEDULE (30 min) --> POST & REVIEW (15 min/day)
```

| Step | Time | Frequency | What Happens |
|------|------|-----------|--------------|
| Step 1: PLAN | 30 min | Every 2 weeks | Select 6-8 concepts, write shot list |
| Step 2: FILM | 2-3 hours | Every 2 weeks | Batch filming session at the property |
| Step 3: EDIT | 1.5-2 hours | Every 2 weeks | Edit 6-8 Reels in CapCut (~15 min each) |
| Step 4: SCHEDULE | 30 min | Every 2 weeks | Upload and schedule all Reels in Meta Business Suite |
| Step 5: POST & REVIEW | 15 min/day | Daily + weekly | Respond to comments, track performance |

### Monthly Time Budget

| Activity | Hours/Month | Notes |
|----------|-------------|-------|
| Planning (2 sessions) | 1 hour | 30 min x 2 |
| Filming (2 sessions) | 4-6 hours | 2-3 hrs x 2 |
| Editing (2 sessions) | 3-4 hours | 1.5-2 hrs x 2 |
| Scheduling (2 sessions) | 1 hour | 30 min x 2 |
| Daily engagement | 3-4 hours | 15 min/day x 20-25 days |
| Weekly review | 1 hour | 15 min x 4 weeks |
| **Total** | **~13-16 hours/month** | **~3-4 hours/week** |

This leaves 6-12 hours/week for all other property management tasks (guest communication, cleaning, pricing, platform management).

## 2. Step 1: PLAN (30 minutes)

### What You Do

Open the Reel Templates Library (deliverables/reel-templates-expanded.md) and select 6-8 concepts for this batch session.

### Planning Checklist

- [ ] Open the Reel Templates Library (reel-templates-expanded.md)
- [ ] Check which concepts you posted in the last 2 weeks (never repeat the same concept back-to-back)
- [ ] Select 6-8 concepts covering all 4 content pillars in the correct ratio:
  - 2 Property Experience (30%)
  - 2 Local Discovery (25%)
  - 1-2 Social Proof (25%)
  - 1 Behind the Scenes (20%)
- [ ] Write a simple shot list on paper or phone notes for each selected concept
- [ ] Check the weather forecast for the next 3 days -- plan outdoor/golden-hour shots for clear days
- [ ] If guests are staying this week, prepare the permission script for Social Proof content
- [ ] Note any seasonal themes to incorporate (holidays, weather changes, local events)

### Content Pillar Quick Reference

| Pillar | Target per Batch | Template Numbers |
|--------|-----------------|------------------|
| Property Experience | 2 Reels | Concepts 1, 2, 6, 7 |
| Local Discovery | 2 Reels | Concepts 3, 9, 10 |
| Social Proof | 1-2 Reels | Concepts 4, 8, 11 |
| Behind the Scenes | 1 Reel | Concept 5 |

### Anti-Fatigue Rule

Never use the same concept number two batches in a row. If you posted "Manha no Sitio" (Concept 1) last batch, use "POV: Voce Reservou" (Concept 2) or "O que R$280 Te Oferece" (Concept 6) for Property Experience this batch.

## 3. Step 2: FILM (2-3 hours)

### What You Do

Film all 6-8 Reels in a single concentrated session. Film by LOCATION (not by concept) to minimize equipment movement.

### Equipment

- iPhone (portrait mode, 1080p at 30fps, grid ON)
- Tripod or selfie stick for stabilization (R$30-50 investment)
- Natural lighting only -- no ring lights or professional equipment needed

### Best Times to Film

| Time | Best For | Concepts |
|------|----------|----------|
| Sunrise (6:00-7:30 AM) | "Manha no Sitio", garden, pool reflections | 1, 7 |
| Morning (7:00-9:00 AM) | Nature shots, property exterior, walkthrough | 2, 5, 6 |
| Midday (10:00 AM - 2:00 PM) | Pool activities, BBQ, interior | 4, 6, 11 |
| Golden Hour (5:00-6:30 PM autumn / 4:30-5:30 PM winter) | Sunset, warm tones, atmospheric shots | 1, 2, 7, 10 |

### Filming Route (by Location)

Film in this order to minimize moving equipment:

**Stop 1: Pool Area (30 min)**
- [ ] Wide shot of full pool area
- [ ] Water surface reflections (morning or golden hour)
- [ ] Close-up details: pool edge, loungers, towels
- [ ] Someone using the pool (if guests available and permitted)
- [ ] Pool area in different lighting angles

**Stop 2: BBQ / Churrasqueira Area (20 min)**
- [ ] Churrasqueira overview shot
- [ ] Food preparation or grilling close-ups (if timing works)
- [ ] Table set for a meal
- [ ] Area from multiple angles

**Stop 3: Garden / Nature (20 min)**
- [ ] Wide garden/nature walk shot
- [ ] Close-ups: flowers, trees, birds, insects
- [ ] Path or walkway through the property
- [ ] Green spaces and hammock area

**Stop 4: Interior Rooms (20 min)**
- [ ] POV walking through entrance
- [ ] Bedrooms: made beds, fresh towels, pillows
- [ ] Kitchen and common areas
- [ ] Bathroom details (clean, styled)

**Stop 5: Exterior / Entrance (15 min)**
- [ ] Property entrance and gate
- [ ] Full property wide shot from the best angle
- [ ] Driveway or approach shot
- [ ] Any unique exterior features

**Stop 6: Local Area (30-45 min, optional -- combine with errands)**
- [ ] Driving shots toward wineries or restaurants
- [ ] Quick clips at 2-3 nearby attractions
- [ ] Scenic views from the road
- [ ] Signs or landmarks for Sao Roque

### Filming Rules

- Capture 8-12 raw clips minimum (overshoot by 50% since not all clips will be usable)
- Each clip: 5-15 seconds of raw footage
- Film VERTICAL (9:16 portrait) for all Reel content
- Film horizontal versions of key beauty shots (for potential website or ad use)
- Film 2-3 takes of each shot and pick the best in editing
- Avoid digital zoom -- move closer instead
- Lock exposure by tapping and holding on screen

## 4. Step 3: EDIT (1.5-2 hours, ~15 minutes per Reel)

### What You Do

Edit 6-8 Reels using CapCut free tier. Each Reel takes approximately 15 minutes.

### Tool: CapCut Free Tier

CapCut is a free video editing app that handles cuts, text overlays, transitions, and audio without watermarks on the free tier.

### Editing Workflow per Reel

1. **Import clips** from your phone's "Girassol Reels - [Month]" folder
2. **Trim and arrange** clips to 15-30 seconds total
3. **Add text overlay for the hook** (first 3 seconds) -- use the hook text from the template
4. **Add music/audio** -- use trending audio or appropriate background music from CapCut's free library
5. **Add transitions** between clips (keep them simple: cut, fade, or swipe)
6. **Preview the full Reel** -- does the hook grab attention in the first 3 seconds?
7. **Check for watermarks** -- preview the export to make sure no Pro watermark appears
8. **Delete the CapCut end card** before final export (CapCut adds a branded outro -- remove it)
9. **Export at 1080p vertical (9:16)** -- highest quality available on free tier

### WARNING: CapCut Free Tier Pitfalls

- **ONLY use templates and effects marked "Free"** in CapCut. Pro templates embed permanent watermarks on exported videos even if you preview without them.
- **Always preview the export before saving.** Look for watermarks in corners or at the end of the video.
- **Delete the CapCut end card** (branded outro) before final export every time.
- **Stick to basic features:** cuts, text overlays, free music, transitions. These core features work without watermarks.
- If you see a "Pro" badge on any template, effect, or music track -- do NOT use it.

## 5. Step 4: SCHEDULE (30 minutes)

### What You Do

Upload all 6-8 edited Reels to Meta Business Suite and schedule them for the next 2 weeks.

### Tool: Meta Business Suite (Free)

Meta Business Suite is Meta's free tool for managing Instagram and Facebook content. Available on desktop (business.facebook.com) and as a mobile app.

### Scheduling Workflow

1. Open Meta Business Suite (desktop or mobile app)
2. Go to **Content** > **Create Post/Reel**
3. Upload the first edited Reel from your phone
4. **Add caption** -- copy/paste from the template in reel-templates-expanded.md and customize as needed
5. **Add hashtags** -- copy/paste the appropriate set from the template (5-10 hashtags per post)
6. **Select "Schedule"** instead of "Post Now"
7. **Set posting date and time:**
   - Post on your fixed days (e.g., Mon/Wed/Fri/Sun)
   - Post at consistent times: 7-9 AM or 6-8 PM Brasilia time
8. Repeat for all 6-8 Reels

### LIMITATION: Meta Business Suite Scheduling

- **Maximum scheduling window:** 29 days ahead. You cannot schedule content more than 29 days in the future.
- **Maximum Reel length for scheduling:** 90 seconds. If any Reel exceeds 90 seconds, you must post it manually at the intended time.
- **Workaround for 90+ second Reels:** Set a phone alarm for the intended posting time and post manually through Instagram.

## 6. Step 5: POST and REVIEW (15 min/day ongoing)

### What You Do

Engage with your audience daily and review performance weekly.

### Daily Tasks (15 minutes)

- [ ] Check Instagram for new comments on recent Reels
- [ ] Respond to ALL comments within 2 hours during operating hours (8 AM - 10 PM)
- [ ] Reply to any DMs (Direct Messages) about booking inquiries
- [ ] If someone asks about availability, direct them to WhatsApp or the Airbnb listing link
- [ ] Like and reply to comments on other local accounts (wineries, restaurants, tourism pages) -- this builds community and visibility

### Weekly Review Tasks (15 minutes, same day as ad performance review)

Check Instagram Insights for each Reel posted this week:

- [ ] **Reach:** Is total reach growing week over week? Note the number: _________
- [ ] **3-second hold rate:** Target > 40%. If a concept drops below 40%, flag it for retirement.
  - Reel 1 (concept ___): ___% hold rate
  - Reel 2 (concept ___): ___% hold rate
  - Reel 3 (concept ___): ___% hold rate
  - Reel 4 (concept ___): ___% hold rate
- [ ] **Saves and shares:** Identify Reels with above-average saves or shares -- these indicate high-quality content worth repurposing as ad creative
- [ ] **Top performer check:** Did any Reel get > 2x average reach? If yes, flag it for ad creative repurposing in the Meta Ads funnel (Layer 1)
- [ ] **Underperformer check:** Did any concept get < 50% of average reach? If yes, consider retiring that concept for 4-6 weeks
- [ ] **Profile visits from Reels:** Are people visiting your profile after watching? This is the booking funnel metric.

## 8. Troubleshooting

### "I missed a filming session"

- **Do not panic.** Post any saved clips from previous sessions or behind-the-scenes content from your phone's camera roll.
- Reuse raw footage from the "Girassol Raw" folder with new hooks and captions.
- Ask a current guest (with permission) to share any photos or videos from their stay -- user-generated content is authentic and performs well.
- **Never go dark for more than 3 days.** Even a simple phone video with a quick caption is better than nothing.
- Reschedule the missed filming session for the next available day.

### "My Reels aren't getting views"

1. **Check your hooks** -- are the first 3 seconds visually compelling? The hook is the single most important element.
2. **Try different posting times** -- experiment with morning (7-9 AM) vs. evening (6-8 PM).
3. **Review hashtag relevance** -- are you using hashtags that match what people actually search for?
4. **Check audio** -- Reels with trending audio tend to get more reach. Update your audio choices.
5. **Vary your content** -- if you have been posting mostly Property Experience, shift to Local Discovery or Social Proof.
6. **Look at 3-second hold rates** -- if hold rates are below 40%, your hooks need work. Study which hooks kept viewers past 3 seconds and replicate those patterns.

### "I'm running out of ideas"

- Return to the Reel Templates Library (reel-templates-expanded.md) -- there are 11 concepts to rotate through.
- **Vary the same concept with different conditions:** "Manha no Sitio" on a sunny day vs. a misty morning vs. a rainy day -- same template, completely different feel.
- **Seasonal themes:** Each season changes what activities and visuals are relevant. See Concept 10 (Seasonal Activities).
- **Guest-generated ideas:** Ask guests "O que voce mais gostou?" and film that specific thing.
- **Combine concepts:** A Local Discovery Reel can feature the property at the end, serving dual duty.

### "A concept's engagement is dropping"

- **Content fatigue is real.** If a concept's 3-second hold rate drops below 40% for 2 consecutive weeks, retire it for 4-6 weeks.
- **Rotate templates aggressively.** Never post the same concept number two weeks in a row.
- **Vary the hook** even when using the same concept structure. Different opening text or visual keeps it fresh.
- **Add 2-3 new templates every 2 months** based on what is working. The template library should grow over time.

### "I don't have guests to film for Social Proof content"

- Use written Airbnb reviews instead of live guest footage (see Concept 8: "Avaliacao da Semana").
- Screenshot 5-star reviews and create text-overlay Reels over property b-roll.
- Save all guest testimonial footage to reuse in future batches with fresh editing and captions.
- Shift that week's Social Proof slot to a different pillar temporarily.
