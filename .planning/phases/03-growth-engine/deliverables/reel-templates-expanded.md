## 1. Template Library Overview

This document provides the complete Reel template library for Sitio Girassol's Instagram content. Templates 1-7 were created in Phase 2's Instagram Content Strategy (Section 4). Templates 8-11 are new additions that fill gaps in Social Proof and Local Discovery content pillars.

### Content Pillar Distribution

| Pillar | Templates | Count | % of Library |
|--------|-----------|-------|-------------|
| Property Experience | Concepts 1, 2, 6, 7 | 4 templates | 36% |
| Local Discovery | Concepts 3, 9, 10 | 3 templates | 27% |
| Social Proof | Concepts 4, 8, 11 | 3 templates | 27% |
| Behind the Scenes | Concept 5 | 1 template | 9% (supplemented by variations) |

### How to Use This Library

1. Before each batch filming session, select 6-8 concepts from this library
2. Follow the content pillar ratios: 2 Property Experience, 2 Local Discovery, 1-2 Social Proof, 1 Behind the Scenes
3. Never repeat the same concept number two weeks in a row
4. Each template is designed to be filmed multiple times with different footage -- same structure, fresh content every time
5. Refer to the Content Batch Production Workflow (content-batch-workflow.md) for the full production process

## 3. NEW Concept 8: "Avaliacao da Semana" (Review of the Week)

| Element | Details |
|---------|---------|
| **Pillar** | Social Proof |
| **Duration** | 15-20 seconds |
| **Music** | Warm, positive instrumental -- something that feels trustworthy and inviting |
| **Estimated Filming Time** | 10 minutes (mostly b-roll of mentioned areas) |
| **Repeatable Variation** | Different Airbnb review each week -- maintain a list of best reviews to rotate |

### Hook (First 3 Seconds)

Text overlay showing a real 5-star review snippet displayed over beautiful b-roll of the property. The text creates immediate social proof while the visual shows what the review describes.

Example hook text: "'O melhor fim de semana que ja tivemos!' -- Hospede no Airbnb"

### Shot List

| Time | Shot | Description |
|------|------|-------------|
| 0-3s | Hook | Text overlay with review quote over a property beauty shot (pool, garden, or sunset) |
| 3-8s | Feature shot | The specific area mentioned in the review. If the review mentions the pool, show the pool. If it mentions the BBQ, show the churrasqueira. Match the visual to the words. |
| 8-13s | Experience shot | Guest-style experience shot of that area -- not empty, but showing what it feels like to BE there (water rippling, fire crackling, birds singing) |
| 13-18s | End card | Property wide shot with text: "Venha viver essa experiencia" and "Link na bio" |

### Caption Template

> "Obrigado [GUEST NAME or 'nosso hospede'] pela avaliacao! '[REVIEW EXCERPT - 1-2 sentences max]'. Reserve sua estadia pelo link na bio."

Example:
> "Obrigado pela avaliacao! 'Lugar incrivel, piscina limpa, churrasqueira perfeita. Voltaremos com certeza!' Reserve sua estadia pelo link na bio."

### Hashtags

```
#sitiogirassol #avaliacao #review #5estrelas #hospedagem #saoroque #sitio #piscina #alugueldetemporada
```

### Production Notes

- **Source reviews:** Screenshot your best Airbnb reviews and keep them in a phone folder "Girassol Reviews"
- **Privacy:** Use first names only. If the guest's name is unique or identifiable, use "nosso hospede" instead
- **Best reviews to feature:** Those that mention specific amenities (pool, BBQ, nature, location) because you can match the visual
- **Filming is minimal:** You need b-roll of the property areas, which you likely already have from other concepts. This template is mostly an editing exercise.

## 4. NEW Concept 9: "Rota do Vinho em Sao Roque" (Wine Route)

| Element | Details |
|---------|---------|
| **Pillar** | Local Discovery |
| **Duration** | 20-30 seconds |
| **Music** | Upbeat, sophisticated -- something that evokes a leisurely afternoon of wine tasting |
| **Estimated Filming Time** | 1-2 hours (requires visiting a winery -- combine with a personal trip) |
| **Repeatable Variation** | Feature a different winery each time. Sao Roque has 30+ wineries on the Rota do Vinho. |

### Hook (First 3 Seconds)

Text overlay: "Voce sabia que Sao Roque tem a maior rota de vinhos do Brasil?" with quick cuts of wine glasses, vineyard views, and cheese boards.

### Shot List

| Time | Shot | Description |
|------|------|-------------|
| 0-3s | Hook | Wine close-up (glass being poured or swirled) with text overlay question |
| 3-10s | Arrival | Driving up to or arriving at a winery -- exterior shot, entrance sign, vineyard rows |
| 10-17s | Experience | Wine tasting moment -- clinking glasses, examining wine color, walking between barrels |
| 17-24s | Food | Food pairing shot -- cheese board, pasta, or local dish at the winery restaurant |
| 24-30s | End card | Property exterior with text: "E o Sitio Girassol fica a 15 min da Rota do Vinho. Link na bio." |

### Caption Template

> "A Rota do Vinho de Sao Roque tem mais de 30 vinicolas e o Sitio Girassol e a base perfeita para explorar. Dica: reserve um fim de semana e visite 3-4 vinicolas com calma. Qual vinicola voce quer conhecer? Link na bio para reservar sua estadia."

### Hashtags

```
#sitiogirassol #rotadovinho #saoroque #vinicola #vinho #turismo #fimdesemana #enoturismo #natureza
```

### Production Notes

- **Combine filming with personal visits:** Do not make a special trip just for content. Film when you visit a winery yourself or take guests.
- **One winery per Reel:** Each video features a single winery, giving you 30+ unique Reels from this template alone.
- **Get winery permission:** Ask staff "Posso filmar um pouquinho para o Instagram?" -- most wineries encourage social media exposure.
- **Tag the winery:** Tagging @[winery_handle] in the Reel can lead to them sharing your content with their audience (free reach).
- **Seasonal relevance:** Wine tourism peaks in winter (Jun-Sep) when people seek cozy indoor experiences -- this template is especially powerful during low season.

## 5. NEW Concept 10: "Atividades por Temporada" (Seasonal Activities)

| Element | Details |
|---------|---------|
| **Pillar** | Local Discovery |
| **Duration** | 15-25 seconds |
| **Music** | Season-appropriate: warm acoustic for autumn, cozy/festive for winter, bright/energetic for spring/summer |
| **Estimated Filming Time** | 15-20 minutes (use existing property footage with seasonal framing) |
| **Repeatable Variation** | New seasonal content every quarter. Same template structure, different activities and visuals per season. |

### Hook (First 3 Seconds)

Seasonal text hook that changes quarterly:

| Season | Months | Hook Text | Visual |
|--------|--------|-----------|--------|
| Autumn (Outono) | Mar-Jun | "O que fazer no interior no outono" | Warm-tone footage, falling leaves, cozy fire |
| Winter (Inverno) | Jun-Sep | "Fuja do frio -- e mentira, venha curtir o frio" | Fireplace, hot drinks, blankets, mist |
| Spring (Primavera) | Sep-Dec | "A primavera no interior e assim" | Flowers, green, birds, bright mornings |
| Summer (Verao) | Dec-Mar | "Verao no sitio: piscina o dia inteiro" | Pool, water splashes, cold drinks, sunshine |

### Shot List

| Time | Shot | Description |
|------|------|-------------|
| 0-3s | Hook | Seasonal text overlay with matching visual (see table above) |
| 3-10s | Activity 1 | First seasonal activity. Autumn: bonfire/churrasco. Winter: vin quente/fireplace. Spring: hiking/garden. Summer: pool/water games. |
| 10-17s | Activity 2 | Second seasonal activity. Autumn: wine tasting. Winter: fondue/soups. Spring: winery visit. Summer: BBQ with cold beer. |
| 17-22s | Property fit | Property shot showing how the sitio fits the season. Pool in summer, fire pit in winter, garden in spring, warm-tone exterior in autumn. |
| 22-25s | End card | "Reserve seu [season] no Sitio Girassol. Link na bio." |

### Caption Templates (Per Season)

**Autumn:**
> "Outono no interior: fogueira, vinho, e natureza. O Sitio Girassol e perfeito para curtir o clima ameno. Reserve seu fim de semana pelo link na bio."

**Winter:**
> "Inverno no sitio: vin quente, lareira, e aquela tranquilidade que so o interior oferece. Traga a familia! Link na bio."

**Spring:**
> "Primavera no Sitio Girassol: flores, passaros, piscina voltando a aquecer. A melhor epoca para curtir a natureza. Link na bio."

**Summer:**
> "Verao no sitio: piscina o dia inteiro, churrasco a tarde, e estrelas a noite. Reserve antes que lote! Link na bio."

### Hashtags

Rotate per season:

**Autumn:** `#sitiogirassol #outono #interior #saoroque #fogueira #natureza #fimdesemana #turismo`
**Winter:** `#sitiogirassol #inverno #frio #saoroque #lareira #escapadosinverno #natureza #turismo`
**Spring:** `#sitiogirassol #primavera #flores #saoroque #natureza #piscina #fimdesemana`
**Summer:** `#sitiogirassol #verao #piscina #saoroque #ferias #churrasqueira #natureza`

### Production Notes

- **Film seasonal footage throughout the year:** When you notice seasonal changes at the property (first autumn leaves, winter mist, spring flowers, summer heat), capture quick clips even outside of batch filming sessions.
- **Low-season content is critical:** May-September is historically the lowest booking period. Winter-themed content ("escape to the country") can drive bookings during this period.
- **Reuse footage across templates:** A sunset shot filmed for Concept 7 can be reused in an autumn Seasonal Activities Reel with different text and framing.
- **Plan ahead:** At the start of each quarter, identify 2-3 seasonal hooks and activities to feature.

## 6. NEW Concept 11: "Depoimento do Hospede" (Guest Testimonial Video)

| Element | Details |
|---------|---------|
| **Pillar** | Social Proof |
| **Duration** | 15-30 seconds |
| **Music** | Soft background music -- low volume so the guest's voice is clearly audible |
| **Estimated Filming Time** | 5 minutes per testimonial (ask during checkout) |
| **Repeatable Variation** | Different guest each time. Every booking is a potential testimonial. |

### Hook (First 3 Seconds)

Guest speaking directly to camera with their genuine first reaction: "A gente amou ficar aqui porque..." -- authentic, unscripted, real emotion.

### Shot List

| Time | Shot | Description |
|------|------|-------------|
| 0-3s | Guest hook | Guest speaking to camera -- their genuine, unscripted opening sentence about what they loved. This is the most powerful hook because authentic emotions are magnetic. |
| 3-13s | Guest + b-roll | Guest continues describing their experience while b-roll of the property plays over their voice. Edit to show what they are describing (if they mention pool, cut to pool footage). |
| 13-18s | Guest recommendation | Guest on camera: "Eu recomendo para..." (families, couples, friends, etc.) with text overlay showing their first name and city. |
| 18-25s | End card | Property beauty shot with text: "Obrigado [NAME]! Reserve sua estadia pelo link na bio." |

### Caption Template

> "[GUEST NAME] de [CITY] conta como foi a experiencia no Sitio Girassol. Obrigado pela visita! Sua vez de viver isso -- reserve pelo link na bio."

Example:
> "Ana e Ricardo de Sao Paulo contam como foi o fim de semana no Sitio Girassol. Obrigado pela visita! Sua vez de viver isso -- reserve pelo link na bio."

### Hashtags

```
#sitiogirassol #depoimento #hospedes #review #saoroque #alugueldetemporada #sitio #experiencia #piscina
```

### Production Notes

- **IMPORTANT: Get explicit verbal permission before filming.** Follow the guest permission process from Phase 2's Instagram Content Strategy (Section 10). Ask during check-in: "Posso filmar um pouquinho da sua estadia para o Instagram? So se voce se sentir confortavel."
- **Best timing to ask for testimonial:** During checkout, when the guest is still in a positive mood from their stay. NOT during check-in (too early, no experience yet).
- **Keep it brief and authentic:** 15-30 seconds maximum. Never script what the guest should say. Prompt them with: "O que voces mais gostaram da estadia?" and let them speak naturally.
- **If the guest is camera-shy:** Ask if they would be comfortable with just their voice over property footage (no face shown). Some guests prefer this and the content is still authentic.
- **Send the Reel to the guest after posting:** Via WhatsApp: "Olha como ficou! Obrigado por serem parte do nosso conteudo." This often leads to the guest sharing it (free reach) and leaving a positive Airbnb review.
- **Build a testimonial library:** Even if you do not post every testimonial immediately, save them all. You can edit and post them in future batches when you need Social Proof content.

### Rotation Rules

1. **Never use the same concept number two weeks in a row** -- if you posted Concept 1 in Week 1, do not post Concept 1 in Week 2
2. **Never post the same pillar two days in a row** -- alternate between pillars across the week
3. **Each concept can repeat monthly** -- the same concept with fresh footage is new content
4. **Social Proof concepts (4, 8, 11) can alternate weekly** -- you likely will not have guest footage every week, so rotate between review-based (8) and testimonial-based (11)
5. **Behind the Scenes (Concept 5) appears 1-2x per month** -- it is the lowest priority pillar
6. **Seasonal Activities (Concept 10) appears 1-2x per month** -- more during season transitions

### Retirement Rules

- If a concept's 3-second hold rate drops **below 40%** for 2 consecutive weeks, **retire it for 4-6 weeks**
- A "retired" concept stays in the library but is not used in the rotation
- After 4-6 weeks, bring the retired concept back with a significantly different hook (new text, new opening visual)
- If a concept fails again after returning, permanently retire it and replace it with a new template

### Adding New Templates

Plan to add 2-3 new templates every 2 months based on performance data. When creating new templates, follow this format:

**Template Requirements (per D-20):**
- Concept name (in Portuguese)
- Content pillar assignment
- Duration target (15-30 seconds)
- Hook description (first 3 seconds -- this is the most important element)
- Shot list with time markers
- Caption template (in Portuguese)
- Hashtag set (5-10 relevant hashtags)
- Estimated filming time
- Repeatable variation notes (how to film it differently each time)

**Where to find new concept ideas:**
1. Look at your top-performing Reels -- what made them work? Create a template around that pattern.
2. Check what competitors are posting (other sitios, pousadas, Airbnbs in Sao Roque region)
3. Follow Instagram trends (trending audio, trending formats) and adapt them to your property
4. Ask guests what content they would want to see before booking
