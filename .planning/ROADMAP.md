# Roadmap: Sitio Girassol Revenue Maximization

## Overview

Three phases over 5 weeks to hit R$4,000 by April 30 and R$7,000 by May 31. Phase 1 fixes the Airbnb foundation this week (pricing floor, listing optimization, guest templates) because every downstream marketing dollar lands on this listing. Phase 2 expands distribution to Booking.com and Brazilian platforms while launching restructured ads and Instagram content. Phase 3 layers the advanced ad funnel and content production system that sustain revenue through low season. The owner has R$2,000+ already confirmed for April and 10-15 hours/week to execute.
